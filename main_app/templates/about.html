About Us

Born from a shared passion for crafting unforgettable experiences, Glow was founded by visionary industry leaders who met beneath the radiance of a legendary nightclub. With a mission to redefine luxury and exclusivity, Avery Thompson, Lunar Rodriguez, and Priya Patel formed an indomitable team. Our unrivaled network, built on years of cultivating relationships, unlocks doors and conjures magical moments that are simply unparalleled.

For milestone celebrations, corporate events, or intimate gatherings, Glow transforms your vision into breathtaking reality. Embark on an enchanting journey where luxury, exclusivity, and unforgettable moments intertwine. Discover the brilliance of Glow and immerse yourself in an extraordinary nightlife experience that will leave you in awe.

Lunar Rodriguez
Atmosphere
Lunar Rodriguez, the Style & Atmosphere Specialist and co-founder of Glow, is a creative force behind the company's distinctive aesthetic. Their unique perspective and eye for detail have shaped some of the most remarkable events in the industry. Lunar's expertise lies in creating visually stunning experiences that leave a lasting impression. Recognized for their outstanding contributions, they received the 2022 Luminary Award for Innovative Event Styling. With a passion for pushing boundaries, Lunar continues to set new standards of style and ambiance in the party planning realm.
Avery Thompson
Culture
Avery Thompson is the Culture Specialist and co-founder of Glow. With a passion for creating immersive experiences, Avery has been deeply involved in the world of nightlife since his early days at the renowned Truth Nightclub. Throughout his career, he has successfully managed appearances for artists including the likes of electrifying talents such as Melody Beatrice and Jaxon Steele. Avery's innate understanding of diverse cultures, combined with his unparalleled industry knowledge, allows him to infuse every event with an infectious energy that captivates attendees.
Priya Patel
Exclusivity
Introducing Priya Patel, the Accommodations and Exclusivity Specialist and co-founder of Glow. Drawing from her experience as an executive assistant at successful start-ups such as QoolTow and Skram, as well as her expertise in major event planning, Priya brings a wealth of knowledge and precision to her role. She maintains an extensive network of connections and industry relationships which enables her to curate exclusive experiences and secure premium accommodations for Glow's clientele. With Priya at the helm, every client can expect nothing less than a truly exceptional and tailored event experience.
